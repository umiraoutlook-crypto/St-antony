Intellectual Rigor & Holistic Growth

At St. Antony’s, we follow the Tamil Nadu State Board curriculum (Samacheer Kalvi) for classes VI to XII. Our primary goal is to foster an environment of conceptual clarity, scientific reasoning, and analytical thinking. We ensure that our students do not just memorize facts, but deeply understand the application of knowledge.

Our classroom experience is enhanced by modern smart-boards, digital labs, and individual attention. With a low student-to-teacher ratio, our educators are able to support and challenge each student, ensuring that every girl achieves her potential.

We place high emphasis on regular assessments, remedial coaching for students needing extra support, and specialized training programs for competitive entrance exams.

Course Curricula & Streams

Structured learning pathways designed for primary, secondary, and higher secondary sections

Secondary Education

Grades VI - X

Focuses on establishing strong fundamentals in core subjects. Prepare students for the secondary school leaving certificate examinations (SSLC).

Core Languages

  • Language I: English (Communicative & Literature)
  • Language II: Tamil / French

Core Subjects

  • Mathematics (Algebra, Geometry, Arithmetic)
  • Science (Physics, Chemistry, Biology with Lab Practical sessions)
  • Social Science (History, Civics, Geography, Economics)

Higher Secondary

Grades XI - XII

Specialized streams preparing young girls for college and professional careers. Heavy emphasis on practical, hands-on lab sessions.

Language Options

Beyond the Classroom

Cultivating leadership, creativity, and fitness through vibrant co-curricular clubs

Sports & Athletics

Our students participate and secure medals in State and Zonal Level Athletics, Basketball, and Volleyball tournaments. Physical training is a mandatory part of our weekly calendar.

Cultural & Fine Arts

From classical Bharatanatyam to modern dance, theater, debating, and vocal music, the school cultural society provides a stage for creative expression and public performance.

Clubs & Societies

Active student participation in the Interact Club (community service), Eco Club (sustainability), Literary & Debate Club, and National Cadet Corps (NCC) to build character.
