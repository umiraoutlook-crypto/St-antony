Enrollment Process

How to Join St. Antony's

We strive to make our enrollment process transparent, simple, and straightforward for parents and candidates. Follow the structured steps below to submit your inquiry and confirm admission.

1

Submit Online Inquiry

Fill out the online admission inquiry form on this page with the required details. Our admissions counselor will review the details and reach out to you via email or phone within 48 business hours to schedule an interaction.

2

Counseling & Interaction

Parents and the candidate will visit the campus for a brief interaction with the Principal and class coordinators. This conversation helps us understand the student's interests, academic background, and expectations.

3

Document Verification

After approval from the Principal, parents must submit physical copies of the required academic transcripts and personal identity documents at the administrative office for official verification.

4

Fee Payment & Enrollment

Complete the payment of term fees at the school accounts desk to secure and finalize the student's seat. Welcome kits, uniforms guidelines, and academic calendars will be provided upon confirmation.

Documents Required at Verification

  • Original Birth Certificate (with 2 photocopies)
  • Transfer Certificate (TC) from the previous school
  • Copy of Student Aadhaar Card
  • Recent Passport Size Photographs (3 copies)
  • Community Certificate (if applicable)

Scholarships & Fee Policies

Our commitment to making quality education accessible to every deserving student

Fee Structure Guidelines

St. Antony's Girls Higher Secondary School operates as a partially-aided, non-profit academic institution. Our fee structure is audited and approved by the State Fee Committee to keep costs affordable while maintaining premium academic infrastructure.

Term fees are payable three times a year (June, October, and January). Textbooks, notebook packages, and school uniforms are separate items. Installment options are available upon written application to the administration office for families facing financial constraints.

Scholarships & Financial Aid

We believe financial hardships should never stand in the way of a girl's education. We offer multiple scholarship models:

  • Academic Topper Scholarship: 100% tuition fee waiver for class board exam toppers.
  • Sports Excellence Waiver: Sports council fee waivers for state and national competitors.
  • Need-Based Concessions: Dedicated trust funds providing aid to single-parent and low-income families.
