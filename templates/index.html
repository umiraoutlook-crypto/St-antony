Message from the Principal

Cultivating Minds, Inspiring Hearts

Welcome to St. Antony’s Girls Higher Secondary School. For over seven decades, our school has stood as a beacon of educational excellence in Chennai. We believe in providing an empowering environment where every young girl is encouraged to explore her potential, voice her ideas, and develop into a confident leader.

Our focus extends beyond classrooms; we blend classical moral foundations with modern scientific inquiry. Here, girls do not just receive degrees, they acquire character, build empathy, and establish friendships that last a lifetime.

"We do not train students merely to pass public examinations, but to navigate the path of life with grace, knowledge, and courage."

Rev. Sr. Maria Stella, M.A., B.Ed.
Principal, St. Antony's Girls H.S.S.
